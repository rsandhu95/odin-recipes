Tacos

Quick and easy to make street style chicken tacos. Packed with flavour and look great!

Ingredients

  • 6 corn tortillas
  • 3 cups chopped cooked chicken
  • 4 sprigs fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 cup chopped white onion
  • 1 cup guacamole
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges

Steps

  1. Place a paper towel on a microwave-safe plate and top with tortillas; heat in the microwave for 10 seconds.
  2. Place chicken in a microwave-safe bowl; heat in the microwave until heated through, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
  3. Layer chicken, cilantro, onion, and guacamole, in that order, onto each tortilla. Squeeze lime juice over each taco.
